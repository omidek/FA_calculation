We are unable to provide the raw data that are part of the Penn State University dataset,
as these data are highly identifiable in nature and are in legal and ethical violation
of the informed consent obtained from the participants. Interested and qualified researchers
may send requests for a more confined sharing of the data to Mark D. Shriver at mds17@psu.edu. 
3D surface images gathered at the University of Pittsburgh for the 3D Facial Norms cohort
are available through the FaceBase Consortium (www.facebase.org) under accession number FB00000491.01,
or at: 
https://www.facebase.org/data/record/#1/isa: dataset/accession=FB00000491.01.
